About Us

Pizza is a dish believed to be of Italian origin. It consists of a usually round, flat base of leavened wheat-based dough.
The base is normally topped with cheese and often various other ingredients such as meat, pepperoni, mushrooms, mixed veggies.
This is then baked at a high temperatures.

At Casa Della Pizza, we have perfected the art of pizza baking. The pizzas are delicious and leave one craving for more.
